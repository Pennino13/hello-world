Guideline for good cards

1. Take a look at other cards, that were approved aka are in play stack via Play_ENG or Play_GER.

2. Also consider to read out your card to a friend to get an idea about the appropriate difficulty.

3. After you posted your card, make sure to regularly check for feedback, which will be displayed under My Cards. Maybe you want to edit the card according to the feedback given.
